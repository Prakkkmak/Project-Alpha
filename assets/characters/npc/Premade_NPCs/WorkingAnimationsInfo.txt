Most of the premade characters also include 'working' animations. 
These layers are compatible with the animated workbenches included in Minifantasy - Crafting And Professions I and II

+ Crafting And Professions I
- Alchemist: Laboratory
- Blacksmith: Anvil
- Carpenter: Wood Workshop
- Jeweller: Jewelry Workshop

+ Crafting And Professions II
- Butcher: Preparation Table
- Cooker: Kitchen
- Dyer: Dye Workshop
- Furrier: Leather Workshop
